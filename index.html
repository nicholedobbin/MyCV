My Journey


about me_

I'm an aspiring junior developer with a background in education, puzzle enthusiast and avid traveler. I love exploring new places, and I've been lucky enough to experience a diverse range of countries and cultures throughout my career, having spent fifteen years living and working in the UK, Egypt and Vietnam.

I'm currently completing a full-stack software and web-development course through HyperionDev. When I complete this, I hope to combine my tech skills with my experience and knowledge of the education sector to help bring about space and change in mainstream education.

I'm also a pretty decent origamist.

experience_

Before my leap into the world of tech, I was an early years and primary school teacher living and working internationally. After thirteen years of teaching across a range of mainstream and alternative education models, I became curious about self-directed education and how ed-tech could support it, and took a year's sabbatical to research this.

In my curiosity and research, I discovered my passion for coding. I love learning new skills and am motivated by anything creative or puzzle related, so I decided to combine these passions and discover what I can do as a developer.
